Accepted are short films done in the stylistic of black and white silent film. Priority in the selection is given to films, which in one way or another the theme of apocalypse is disclosed. Works are accepted , regardless of the time of their production.

The final screening will be held on the Night of museums, May 19th, 2012, at the National Art Museum of Belarus. The winner is determined by votes of the audience.

The best film will be automatically included in the competition program of the International Film Festival Cinema Perpetuum Mobile.

Each film must be accompanied by an application, filled in a free form.

The application must specify:

  • The authors (creators) of the film
  • The name in the the native and English languages
  • The country of production
  • The year of production
  • Duration of the film

The works and applications are accepted by mail, in person or through the Internet. The movie file should be encoded DVD-format.

The works are to be submitted until May 5th, 2012.
